Effective Date: March 26, 2026
Last Updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dr. Ozkose Chiropractic Business Coaching ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with us via Instagram Direct Messages.

1. Information We Collect

When you send us a Direct Message on Instagram, we may collect:

  • Your Instagram username and public profile information
  • The content of messages you send to us
  • Information you voluntarily share (e.g. practice size, goals, challenges)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about our chiropractic business coaching services
  • Determine whether our coaching program is a good fit for your practice
  • Follow up with you regarding your interest in our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Instagram Platform Data

Our application accesses Instagram messaging data through the Meta/Instagram API solely to facilitate direct communication with people who reach out to us. We access only the messages sent directly to our Instagram Business account. We do not access your personal Instagram account, followers, or any data beyond our direct message thread with you.

4. Data Storage

Message data and lead information collected through Instagram DMs is stored locally and securely on our private systems. We retain this information only as long as necessary to manage our coaching intake process. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse. Access to stored data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of further communication at any time by simply letting us know in the DM thread

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for chiropractic practice owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via Instagram DM at @dr.ozkose.
